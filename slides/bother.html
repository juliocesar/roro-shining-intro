Why bother?

Shining is for presentations what Rails is for web apps, though a lot smaller, and hopefully it'll stay like that. Presentations (in general), are, in a nutshell, images and words, and sometimes *cough* audio right?

That's what you get when you use Shining. Plus all the awesomeness available in HTML5 (e.g.: native audio/video). Also, things look really awesome when rendered in Webkit.
